About Me

I'm a producer from BC, Canada who's had a passion for making music as long as I can remember.

I first got into production when my parents got me FL Studio at the age of 12, though I'd been messing around in GarageBand for at least a couple years by that point. I wanted to make songs just like my favourite dubstep musician at the time: Xtrullor (if you don't know who that is, go check him out on Newgrounds).

Though I started making dubstep, my style evolved over time, and I started making hardcore techno, hard dance, and most recently idol pop, as well as composing orchestral and concert band pieces from time to time.

Originals and Remixes

Psellism

I made this track a fairly long time ago based off of the album art, itself a drawing by a YouTube animator named Atlas Crossman of a character Dillon from their series Boku no Akuma. While a big deviation from my (mostly unreleased) style at the time, it was a very fun track to make.

Kiss Me Again (Sl1pz Remix)

Cover art by spookynya. I really like the original of this song, so I decided to remix it one day and came up with this. I quite enjoy how it turned out, especially the kick in the video, since I'd never designed one like that before.

Risukami

I would be remiss if I didn't pay homage to Nyan~ Neko Sugar Girls, a YouTube animated series about a couple of neko girls, one of whom was drawn on the cover by spookynya. I watched the series first in band camp when I was 14 and it's changed the trajectory of my life more than I ever thought it would. Something something brostep banger.
